Glimpse of Char Dham Yatra 2023

Explore the Divine Char Dham Yatra Itinerary

Embark on a soul-stirring journey of spirituality and transcendence with our exclusive Char Dham Yatra. Nestled in the majestic Himalayas, this pilgrimage offers a unique blend of convenience, spiritual enrichment, and breathtaking natural beauty. Our meticulously crafted itinerary ensures a seamless and enriching experience for every traveler.

Dehradun to Yamunotri:

The journey commences in Dehradun, where you will be greeted with warmth and prepared for the divine odyssey ahead. The highlight of this leg is the helicopter ride that whisks you away to Yamunotri, the sacred source of the Yamuna River. Surrounded by pristine Himalayan vistas, Yamunotri is home to the revered Yamunotri Temple. The rejuvenating hot water springs of "Surya Kund" and "Gauri Kund" add to the spiritual experience.

Gangotri:

Your pilgrimage continues with a helicopter journey to Gangotri, the birthplace of the holy Ganges River. Here, you will be captivated by the mesmerizing Ganges aarti, a spiritual ritual that connects you with the sacred river's divine energy. The Gangotri Temple, dedicated to Goddess Ganga, stands as a testament to faith and devotion, nestled amidst the awe-inspiring Himalayan peaks.

Kedarnath:

Kedarnath, the abode of Lord Shiva, beckons next. The helicopter ride ensures swift access to the hallowed Kedarnath Temple. Situated at an altitude of 3,583 meters, this sacred shrine offers an unparalleled spiritual atmosphere. Engage in meditation and spiritual classes to deepen your connection with the divine. The serenity of the surroundings amplifies the sense of tranquility and devotion.

Badrinath:

The final leg of your Char Dham Yatra takes you to Badrinath, the dwelling place of Lord Vishnu. With VIP darshans, you can revel in an unhurried and intimate connection with the deity. The Badrinath Temple, nestled in the Garhwal Himalayas, exudes an aura of divinity. The pilgrimage culminates with a profound sense of inner peace and spiritual fulfillment.
